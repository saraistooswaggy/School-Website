The International Indian School Buraidah was established as a branch of The Embassy of Indian School, Riyadh on 26 August 1989 with 45 students and 9 teachers under the guidance of H E Talal Hussain Al Beyari, The Director General of Health Affairs,Qassim Province and through the dedicated efforts of the Indian Community in Buraidah.

Welcome To IIS

The School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi, India and is licensed under the Ministry of Education, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The School endeavors to move towards fulfillments of above goals under the kind patronage and able guidance of Mr Hemant Kotalwar Charge d' Affaires and Deputy Chief of Mission, Embassy of India, Riyadh. Today the strength of students is above 1000, 66 teachers, 7 administrative staff and 10 supporting staff . Being the only School in the Al Qassim Region, IISB takes great pride in the knowledge that our students travel within a range of 150 kms to attend school, as ours is the only Institution committed to the noble cause of imparting education to Indians in this province.
